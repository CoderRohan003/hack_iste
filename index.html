About Us

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional nonprofit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System operating in association with All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and other International Projects. We are the Oldest Students’ Chapter of Eastern India.


We are excited to present "NITDGP Hacks 1.0", an offline hackathon with a prime focus on college going students in and outside NIT Durgapur. This event promises to be a great learning and immersive experience, combining live coding, brainstorming, and building with the team in a very short span. Unlike many hackathons where there’s an unfair advantage to those having a vast hackathon experience, we aim to set a platform for freshmen to learn and spark their curiosity in hackathons, by making this one of their best first-hackathons. This highly acclaimed offline event of our society, is open to students across all streams in and out of NIT Durgapur.
